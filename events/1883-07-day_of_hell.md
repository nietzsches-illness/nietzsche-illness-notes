# July 1883: Day of hell

> Recently, he had endured a ‘day of hell’, followed by a nervous collapse,
> when he was informed by the manager of the Hétel Edelweiss that the entire
> Rée family were expected to turn up in Sils-Maria that very afternoon.
> Drenched to the skin by a cloudburst, he had hurried to the post-station to
> book a seat on the next day’s stagecoach headed for Chur  . only to disover a
> few hours later that the family name of the newcomers had been wrongly
> registered.

[@cate2005friedrich, p. 414]
